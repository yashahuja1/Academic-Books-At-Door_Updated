Academic Books At Door

We are going to develop a website that provides the smarter functioning of library.

Many students find it difficult to get perfect book from local store.

SOME PROBLEMS FACED BY STUDENTS:
1> Not getting required edition of book.
2> Have to pay some extra amount to buy fresh books from local store because of less stock.
3> Getting damaged book while buying used books.
4> Some pages lost in used books.

So we decide to solve this problem of students by developing this website.
If student buy new book from our website he or she gets particular edition used by their faculties.
And if student buy used books from seniors using this website we provides verified book feature to not get damaged or lost page book.
